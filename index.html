Role definition

1440 Advisory Group provides clinically aligned advisory, coordination, and continuity services designed to operate alongside licensed medical, psychiatric, and therapeutic providers.

Our role is to provide structure, accountability, integration, and continuity in complex, high-risk situations—without delivering clinical treatment or replacing licensed care.

How contact works

Initial contact is referral-based and exploratory. We prioritize clarity, fit, and scope alignment before any engagement is considered.

Message guidance

  • Briefly describe the situation and why continuity or coordination risk is elevated.
  • Confirm whether licensed medical, psychiatric, or therapeutic care is currently in place (if applicable).
  • Clarify who holds decision authority and whether consent can be established.
  • Indicate the desired outcome of an initial alignment conversation (e.g., assess fit, define roles, determine next steps).

What not to send

  • clinical records unless requested and appropriate consent is established
  • urgent or emergency messages (see disclaimer below)
  • requests for diagnosis, therapy, prescribing, or medical treatment

Response expectations

  • Responses are exploratory and scope-focused.
  • If fit is unclear, we may decline or recommend appropriate licensed resources.
  • If there is potential fit, we will outline a simple next-step process for role clarity and consent.

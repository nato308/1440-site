Institutional Summary

Professional overview for hospitals, institutions, and high-trust partners.

Role

1440 Advisory Group provides clinically aligned advisory, coordination, and continuity services designed to operate alongside licensed medical, psychiatric, and therapeutic providers.

Our role is intentionally scoped. We do not replace licensed providers. We provide structure, accountability, coordination, and continuity where outcomes most often failâ€”at transitions, handoffs, and points of fragmentation.

The problem we address

  • transitions between levels of care
  • fragmented or conflicting recommendations
  • unmanaged family-system dynamics
  • lack of continuity after discharge or stabilization
  • unclear authority and responsibility
  • privacy and consent breakdowns

When 1440 is appropriate

  • complexity exceeds what any single provider or institution can manage alone
  • continuity between inpatient, outpatient, and home settings is critical
  • multiple providers require alignment
  • family dynamics materially affect outcomes
  • executive or high-discretion circumstances require added structure
  • transitions carry elevated risk

When 1440 is not appropriate

  • licensed clinical care is required and not in place
  • authority and consent cannot be clearly defined
  • expectations involve diagnosis, therapy, or medical treatment
  • collaboration and accountability are not possible

Service architecture

High-level view. Phases are scoped, documented, and time-bounded.

  • Referral Gate & Qualification
  • Integrated Assessment
  • Stabilization & Withdrawal Coordination (when indicated, with licensed providers)
  • Integrated Care-Team Coordination
  • Family Systems Advising & Governance
  • Recovery & Performance Operating System
  • Continuity & Transition Safeguards
